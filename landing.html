Welcome to the membership area of the BSAVA website. Here you will find all you need to know about the benefits of becoming a BSAVA member, the various membership categories, and how to join.

BSAVA membership is here to support you throughout your vetinary career, at whatever stage, so we have tailored membership categories for vets, vet nurses or vet students and it doesn't matter if you are in the UK or overseas, you can still join.

Membership categories

Please note in order to apply for membership online you will need to be registered with the website. if you are not already registered with the website you will be asked to register prior to completing your membership application form.

Full member

You must be a member of. or be eligible for registration with. the RCVS or a veterinary practitioner listed on the Supplementary Register.
